Mega Man

Debut: Mega Man

Serial Number: DLN. 001

Creator: Dr. Light

Weapon: Mega Buster

Weakness: Spikes

Bio: Formerly Dr. Light's helper robot known only as 'Rock'. When Dr. Wily stole and reprogrammed Dr. Light's robot masters for his evil purposes, Rock insisted Dr. Light to reprogram him into a fighting robot. Having no other solution, Dr. Light reluctantly agreed to Rock's request. Thus, the super fighting robot Mega Man was born; living the rest of his life ruining Dr. Wily's plans for world domination and fighting for everlasting peace.
