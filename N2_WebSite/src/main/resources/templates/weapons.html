Mega Buster

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Buster

User: Mega Man

Description: Fires small solar energy pellets. Can be charged up for a larger, more powerful shot that can blaze through weak enemies.

Rolling Cutter

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Metal

User: Cut Man

Description: Throws a cutter that boomerangs forth and back towards the user.

Super Arm

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Earth

User: Guts Man

Description: Allows the user to pick up so-called Guts Blocks and use them as projectiles. Also allows access to areas that are usually blocked by said blocks.

Ice Slasher

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Ice

User: Ice Man

Description: Shoots icy projectiles that freeze enemies in place for a few seconds. Can also freeze pillars of fire.

Hyper Bomb

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Explosive

User: Bomb Man

Description: Lobs an explosive which detonates shortly.

Fire Storm

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Fire

User: Fire Man

Description: Shoots a fiery projectile while also generating a fiery aura around the user for a brief moment.

Thunder Beam

Debut: Mega Man

Element: Electric

User: Elec Man

Description: Shoots electric projectiles forwards, upwards and downwards simultaneously. Projectiles can rip through enemies.

Time Slow

Debut: Mega Man: Powered Up

Element: Temporal

User: Time Man

Description: Slows the flow of time around the user for a short period.

Oil Slider

Debut: Mega Man: Powered Up

Element: Other

User: Oil Man

Description: Shoots a blob of oil that sticks on floors. Oil puddles can be used as 'boards' for fast forward movement, Oil boards can be jumped out of in mid-air for a second jump, but destroys the board in the process.

Metal Blade

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Metal

User: Metal Man

Description: Large saw blades can be fired in different directions and rip through small weak enemies.

Air Shooter

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Air

User: Air Man

Description: Shoots a set of 3 projectlies that quickly rise upwards as soon as they're fired.

Bubble Lead

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Water

User: Bubble Man

Description: Drops a large bubble that moves along the ground.

Quick Boomerang

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Other

User: Quick Man

Description: Small boomerangs that can be fired rapidly.

Crash Bomber

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Explosive

User: Crash Man

Description: Fires a projectile that sticks to walls and explodes shortly, can be used to destroy certain walls. Does not explode when in contact with certain enemies.

Time Stopper

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Temporal

User: Flash Man

Description: Stops time around the user. The effect lasts until the weapon runs out of energy.

Atomic Fire

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Fire

User: Heat Man

Description: Fires small fiery projectiles. Can be charged up for a larger and much more powerful projectile.

Leaf Shield

Debut: Mega Man 2

Element: Earth

User: Wood Man

Description: Generates a barrier around the user that damages enemies. The barrier can be launched at different directions.
