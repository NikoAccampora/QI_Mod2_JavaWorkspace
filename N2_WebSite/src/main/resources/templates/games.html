Mega Man

Release Date: 1987/12/17

Platforms: Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: The evil Dr. Wily has taken Dr. Light's robots and is using them to take over world! It's up to Mega Man to stop him!

Mega Man 2

Release Date: 1988/12/24

Platforms: Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: Dr. Wily is back, and this time he built 8 of his own Robot Masters to destroy Mega Man! Can Mega Man prevail and defeat Dr. Wily once again?

Mega Man 3

Release Date: 1990/09/28

Platforms: Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: Dr. Wily seems to have finally changed and is working with Dr. Light to build Gamma, a giant super robot to protect the world. But they need energy elements from 8 different places. Can Mega Man find them all?

Mega Man: The Wily Wars

Release Date: 1994/10/21

Platforms: Sega Genesis

Story Synopsis: Dr. Wily has built a time machine and travelled back in time to defeat Mega Man! Mega Man must use a time machine of Light's design to travel back to his first three adventures to stop Wily and, finally, take on the Wily Tower!

Mega Man 4

Release Date: 1991/12/06

Platforms: Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: A mysterious scientist named Dr. Cossack has appeared and sent his own Robot Masters to destroy Mega Man! Can Mega Man defeat this new threat?

Mega Man 5

Release Date: 1992/12/04

Platforms: Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: A vicious group of Robots are attacking several parts of the world; and thier leader is... Proto Man!? Mega Man must investigate the situation and learn about his lost brother's true intentions!

Mega Man V

Release Date: 1994/07/22

Platforms: Game Boy

Story Synopsis: A group of vile alien robots known as Stardroids have invaded the Earth and all the other planets of the solar system! It's up to Mega Man to stop them!

Mega Man 6

Release Date: 1993/10/05

Platforms: Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: The first Annual Robot Master tournament has begun, hosted by Mr. X; however... everything goes awry when Mr. X takes control of the most powerful robots and uses them in his favor to try and conquer the world! Mega Man must stop him!

Mega Man 7

Release Date: 1995/03/24

Platforms: Super Nintendo Entertainment System

Story Synopsis: Dr. Wily has escaped prison, with the help of 4 of his Robot Masters to cover his escape. While on the run to stop him, Mega Man encounters Bass, a mysterious humanoid robot who also seems to be after Wily. Can Mega Man really trust this mysterious new ally?

Mega Man 8

Release Date: 1996/12/17

Platforms: Sony Playstation

Story Synopsis: A mysterious robot of alien origin has crashed on Earth; While Mega Man tries to salvage the situation, Dr. Wily sends 4 of his new Robot Masters to attack and bestows them with a mysterious power. Could this power and the alien robot be connected?

Mega Man & Bass

Release Date: 1998/04/24

Platforms: Super Nintendo Entertainment System; Game Boy Advance

Story Synopsis: A mysterious robot who calls himself King has appeared and is starting a robotic revolution against humanity! Mega Man and Bass make thier move to stop him and his servants!

Mega Man: Powered Up

Release Date: 2006/03/02

Platforms: Sony Playstation Portable

Story Synopsis: Relive Mega Man's first adventure, this time with brand new 3D graphics, voice acting and more! You can also play as Proto Man, Roll or one of the Robot Masters(including 2 new ones)!

Mega Man 9

Release Date: 2008/09/22

Platforms: Nintendo Wii, Sony Playstation 3

Story Synopsis: Dr. Light is branded as a criminal and taken away after 8 of his own robots have mysteriously gone rampant! Can Mega Man stop those robots and prove Dr. Light's innocence?

Mega Man 10

Release Date: 2010/03/01

Platforms: Nintendo Wii, Sony Playstation 3

Story Synopsis: A robotic virus called Roboenza is spreading all over the world, causing robots to go berserk! Dr. Wily claims to have a cure, but is stolen by infected robots and asks Mega Man for help. With Proto Man's aid, Mega Man sets out to retrieve the cure with hopes of stopping this viral outbreak.
